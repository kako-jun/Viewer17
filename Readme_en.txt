# Viewer17
================================================================================
Thank you for your interest.

'Viewer17' is a flickable comic viewer for Windows 8 devices.
It also has panels and buttons to move(copy) photos to categorized folders.

It is important to sort manually but not automatically.

- You can enjoy reading books(jpg, png) with iPad-like motions.
- You can sort your photos into multiple folders.
    ex) the purpose of pics

The layout for left-handed is ready.

Sincerely,
The Jpico Team
